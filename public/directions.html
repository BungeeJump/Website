Directions

Central Oregon Bungee Adventures is located 26 miles north of Bend, 136 miles from Portland, and 134 miles from Eugene. The nearest airport is just down the road in Redmond. The site is an easily recognizable 300 foot gorge with three bridge spans.

‍From Bend: Head out of town on North Highway 97. After passing through Terrebonne, PS Ogden State Park is on the left (west), just two miles outside of Terrebonne.

‍From Portland: Take Highway 26 East over Mt Hood through Government Camp. Once in Madras, take Highway 97 south towards Bend. Look for signs for PS Ogden State Park, which will be on the right (west) side of the road. If you make it to Terrebonne you have gone just a couple miles too far.

‍From Eugene: Take Highway 126 East to Sisters, and then Redmond. From Redmond take Highway 97 North for 9 miles. Once through Terrebonne the park will be on your left (west).
